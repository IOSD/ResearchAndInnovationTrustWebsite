About US

Encouraging Present For a Better Future

A resourceful thought and concept intakes intense research to introduce an innovation in the society. A major problem India faces today is the dearth of facilities for conducting research. We, at Research and Innovation Trust, strive to solve the problem providing assistance in raising suitable funds and backing them with technological and managerial support to the innovators.

Objectives

In Service Of Humanity through Science

To foster growth and development in the country by mobilizing funds in the required spheres

To raise the participation level of youth in the progress of the country

To facilitate a ‘call for action’ in order to eradicate the social stigma persisting nationwide

To generate opportunities for enthusiasts, entrepreneurs and thinkers to build upon their ideas

Our Vision

REINVENTING OURSELVES TO THRIVE IN TOMORROW'S WORLD TELL ME MORE

In the surge of technical sphere globally it is still difficult to predict the extent to which science can limit. The ability to reason for the existence of all the elements on the earth leads the mankind in the searchof even new and better living. But behind every discovery there are many hardships and failures too. This courage to conceptualize and forge a structure involves tough struggle to reach to glory. Despite of all the struggle there are some enthusiasts who devote themselves to research for innovations but many are not privileged to avail tools and resources to shape their ideas.

Not only at an individual level, but these new innovations create benefits for the whole society. Therefore, it becomes the responsibility of the masses to promote development of innovation projects. All of us need to be united to make it possible.

Keeping the ultimate motive in mind, Research and Innovation Trust gathers funds from non- State sources, be it individuals, foundations, charities and other private sources, which need to grow in substantial amounts. The Research and Innovation Trust is set up specifically to engage with the community and encourage its various sectors to embrace the emerging need of supporting researches. We thrive to witness a tomorrow where the importance of research is realized and the scope for innovation is widened. Our optimism will drive us forward on the way to develop the shift in the research process.

Who Can Volunteer?

Will I Be Paid To Be A Volunteer?

Anyone aged between 18 and 80 can volunteer with Research and Innovation Trust provided they are able to fulfil the role description and meet the volunteer requirements.

Research and Innovation Trust does not pay volunteers but will cover reasonable expenses

How Do I Apply?

It is really simple to become a volunteer with Research and Innovation Trust
The first step is to apply to volunteer is to contact us on support@ritrust.in
Then we'll be in touch about opportunities in your area.

If you'd like to apply for a role, we'll contact you. Once you have sent us a mail and your references have been received, you will be invited to meet for an interview with a Research and Innovation Trust team member and attend our mandatory volunteer induction training.

What Skills Do I Need?

How Much Time Do I Have To Give?

The skills needed to volunteer with Research and Innovation Trust will vary with the volunteer role. Many of our roles do not require any specific skills and training will always be provided where required. And of course, volunteering with Research and Innovation Trust is a great way to learn and develop new skills.

Our volunteers roles are generally flexible and allow you to give as much or as little time as you can. If time is short for you then we often require volunteers on an ad hoc basis to support our fundraising and promotional events
